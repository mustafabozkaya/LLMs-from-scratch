- **Classifier-Free Guidance**: Enables trade-off between fidelity and creativity

## How to Use (via Diffusers)

```python
import torch
from diffusers import LTXPipeline
from PIL import Image

# Load the pipeline
pipe = LTXPipeline.from_pretrained(
    "Lightricks/LTX-2.3",
    torch_dtype=torch.float16,
    variant="fp16"  # if available
)
pipe.to("cuda")

# Example 1: Image-to-Video
input_image = Image.open("input_image.jpg")
video_frames = pipe(
    image=input_image,
    prompt="A slow zoom revealing intricate details",  # optional text guidance
    num_frames=24,
    guidance_scale=7.5,
    num_inference_steps=25
).frames

# Example 2: Text-to-Video
```

## Limitations and Considerations

### Technical Limitations
- **Computational Requirements**: Significant VRAM needed for higher resolutions/frame counts
- **Generation Latency**: Not real-time for longer videos; batch processing may be needed
- **Length Constraints**: Practical limits on video duration due to memory and coherence challenges
- **Motion Complexity**: Very complex or rapid motions may be challenging to generate coherently
- **Custom Pipeline**: Requires the specific LTXPipeline from diffusers (not a standard Stable Diffusion variant)

### Quality Considerations
- **Temporal Consistency**: While improved, perfect coherence over long sequences remains challenging
- **Detail Preservation**: Fine details may fluctuate between frames
- **Text Alignment**: Precise interpretation of complex textual prompts can vary
- **Audio-Visual Sync**: For audio-to-video, perfect synchronization may require additional tuning

### Ethical and Safety Concerns
- **Deepfake Potential**: Realistic video generation raises concerns about misuse
- **Copyright Issues**: Generated content may inadvertently resemble copyrighted material
- **Bias Representation**: May reflect biases present in training data
- **Misinformation Risk**: Ability to create convincing fictional scenarios

### Practical Deployment
- **Hardware Needs**: GPU with substantial VRAM (24GB+ recommended for higher quality)
- **Optimization Options**: 
  - Use of xformers or other memory-efficient attention
  - Quantization (int8/fp16)
  - Model distillation for faster inference
  - Chunked processing for longer videos
- **Integration Complexity**: More complex than standard text-to-image pipelines due to multiple modalities
